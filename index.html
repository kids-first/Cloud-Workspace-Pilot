Cloud Workspace Pilot: A Practical Hands-On Introduction to the CFDE Portal and CAVATICA Data Analysis Platform 


Breakout Session Overview


Purpose : Deliver working knowledge of CFDE Portal and CAVATICA Data Analysis Platform to CFDE all-hands workshop attendees.

  • Duration: 45 minutes (5 min Accessing Data Sources, 25 min. Platform, 10 min. Questions)
  • Instructors: Sangeeta Shukla (CHOP/Kids First: Data) and Eric Tobin (Velsera: CAVATICA)

Breakout Session Description

The Cloud Workspace Pilot: A Practical Hands-On Introduction to the CFDE Portal and CAVATICA Data Analysis Platform breakout session, led by Sangeeta Shukla and Eric Tobin, will explore funding opportunities for cloud research and will demonstrate basics of analysis and advanced data interoperability features of CAVATICA. In anticipation of this breakout session, we invite you to explore the   Cloud Workspace Pilot program  and engage with the  CAVATICA platform  and   documentation .  Try creating a CAVATICA account and explore some of the Public Projects and available Datasets!  During the session we will cover funding opportunities for DCC research on CAVATICA and the basics of doing research on CAVATICA.
